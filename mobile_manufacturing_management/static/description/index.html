Mobile Manufacturing & IMEI Quality Management

Odoo 19 end-to-end mobile production workflow

Raw material receiving and IE/QC, production requisition, standard Manufacturing Orders, pre-IMEI QC, IMEI generation/import, barcode-driven QC stages, rework, packaging, Odoo packages, Finished Goods transfer, and box-wise IMEI PDF printing.

Key capabilities

  • Incoming raw material acceptance/rejection with stock movements
  • BoM-based material requisition and approved material issue
  • Unique IMEI 1 as Odoo serial number and optional IMEI 2
  • Automatic IMEI generation with Luhn check digit or CSV import
  • Barcode QC Stage 1 and Stage 2 with closed-loop rework
  • Replacement component consumption during rework
  • Dynamic box capacity and box-wise IMEI report

Important: Configure an officially allocated TAC before commercial production and test the module on UAT before production deployment.
